Canned Fish Cake

Description

Sometimes life gets busy and food takes second place to duty. During these times it is not often easy to eat as healthy as we woudl like. Enter canned fish cake! By using canned fish this recipe becomes cheap, healthy and always at your finger tips

Ingredients

  • 1 Can Mackeral fish in olive oil
  • 1/2 cup plain green lentils
  • 1/4 cup of either bread crumbs or coconut flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon: mayonaise and dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp: salt, paprika, garlic powder, cayenne pepper
  • Oil (of your choice) for frying

Steps

  1. Combine all Ingredients
  2. Heat oil in pan
  3. Form mixture into small patties
  4. Fry patties for 2-3 mintues on each side
  5. Enjoy!
