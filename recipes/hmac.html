Healthy Mac 'n Cheese

Description

Sometimes life gets busy and food takes second place to duty. During these times it is not often easy to eat as healthy as we woudl like. Enter healthy mac n cheese! By combing box mac n cheese with fresh roasted Broccoli you have the best of both worlds-quick and healthy!

Ingredients

  • Chopped Broccoli Heads
  • Box Mac N Cheese
  • Greek Yogurt 5%

Steps

  1. Roast Broccoli
  2. Make Mac n Cheese
  3. Combine broccoli and mac n cheese
  4. Add greek yogurt until you reach your desired creaminess
  5. Enjoy!
