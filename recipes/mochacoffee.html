Mocha Coffee

Description

Mocha Coffee is sure to be your new morning go to. This kind of coffee combines your childhood favortie hot chocolate with the adult staples of caffeine and cofee

Ingredients

  • Black coffee
  • Organic Cocoa Powder
  • Whole milk

Steps

  1. Get empty coffee mug
  2. Fill mug with 1/4 cup of warm milk
  3. Fill mug with up to halway with coffee
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of cocoa and stir until well combined
  5. Fill up mug with more coffee until at desired fill level
  6. Enjoy!
